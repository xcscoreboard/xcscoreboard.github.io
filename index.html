Welcome to Cross Country Scoreboard (xcscoreboard)


If other sports can have a scoreboard, then why can’t Cross Country?

Cross Country Scoreboard is a feature rich tool that makes scoring and timing a cross country race a breeze. With just a couple of laptops (with or without a timing system), your race's individual AND projected team scores can be easily streamed automatically in real-time, during the race, to a web based cross country scoreboard which fans and spectators can enjoy on their mobile devices.
